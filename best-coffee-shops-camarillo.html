Best Coffee Shops in Camarillo (2026)

From a creative local cafe with seasonal drinks to a record lounge and the best cafe de olla in town. The best coffee spots in Camarillo, picked by someone who actually lives here.

By Gabrielle Ridgeway Updated April 2026 ☕ 5 picks 📍 Camarillo, CA
These are my personal picks based on living in Camarillo. I only recommend places I'd send a friend. None of these listings are paid or sponsored.

Camarillo has more going on in the coffee scene than most people realize. These five spots each have their own personality from a cozy cafe with local art to a record lounge you can lose an afternoon in. These are the places locals actually go back to.

Photo via Google Maps
Best Overall
Coffee Cafe

Tree Lounge Coffee

🎨 Local art on the walls 🛋️ Stay as long as you want ☕ Creative menu

If you only try one coffee shop in Camarillo, make it Tree Lounge. It has a relaxed, creative feel with local art on the walls and a menu that goes well beyond the basics. The kind of place you can meet a friend or settle in for a few hours without feeling rushed or like you need to leave.

The seasonal drinks are worth paying attention to they rotate and they're usually really good. A genuine neighborhood coffee shop in the best sense of the phrase.

📢 What to order
Brown sugar latte · Seasonal specialty drinks
Best for meeting a friend, working remotely, or anyone who wants a coffee shop with actual character.

Best Variety
Cafe

Makenna Koffee

📋 Big menu 🕐 Long hours 📍 Near the Outlets

One of the easiest go-to spots in Camarillo. The menu is big, the hours are long, and there is genuinely something for everyone coffee, matcha, smoothies, breakfast. It is especially convenient if you are already near the outlets or need a reliable stop without much thought.

📢 What to order
Flavored latte · Specialty drinks · Matcha
Best for a quick and easy stop, grabbing something before shopping, or anyone who wants a big menu with lots of options.

Best Coffee + Pastries
Cafe + Bakery

Café Ficelle

🥐 Excellent pastries 🕯️ Calm atmosphere ✨ Slightly elevated

A little more cafe than coffee shop, but absolutely worth it. Cafe Ficelle has a calmer, slightly elevated feel good for when you want to slow down rather than rush through a drink. The pastries are genuinely excellent, which sets it apart from most coffee spots in town.

Order the almond croissant. You will not regret it.

📢 What to order
Latte · Almond croissant
Best for a slow morning, a pastry craving, or anyone who wants a coffee experience that feels a step above the usual.

Most Unique
Record Lounge + Cafe

American Pie Records Coffee House

🎵 Part record lounge 🌙 Late-night hangout 🍕 Food available

Not your typical coffee shop and that is exactly the point. Part record lounge, part cafe, part late-night hangout. You can grab a coffee, listen to music, order food, and stay longer than you planned. It has a vibe you are not going to find anywhere else in Camarillo.

Because they also serve food and stay open later than most coffee spots, it works as a full stop rather than just a quick caffeine run. One of the more genuinely interesting places in the city.

📢 What to order
Coffee · Something off the food menu
Best for an evening hangout, music lovers, anyone who wants somewhere with real personality and no rush.

Best Local Favorite
Mexican Bakery + Cafe

Panaderia Lucero

💰 $ 🥐 Fresh pan dulce ☕ Cafe de olla

Simple, affordable, and consistently good. This is where you go for cafe de olla and fresh pan dulce. It is less of a sit-and-work spot and more of a quick, satisfying stop that locals have been going to for years.

If you have never had cafe de olla, this is the place to try it. Spiced, a little sweet, and nothing like the coffee you get anywhere else in town. Grab a concha while you are at it.

📢 What to order
Cafe de olla · Concha · Pan dulce
Best for a quick affordable stop, anyone who loves Mexican pastries, and locals who want something real and unpretentious.
