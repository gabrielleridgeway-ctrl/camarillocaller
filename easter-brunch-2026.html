A casual Easter brunch with specialty menu items alongside their regular offerings, plus mimosa specials starting at $7. No buffet, no fixed price you order what you want. Great if you want a festive Easter meal without committing to a set menu or a big spend.

The Crown
$$$ Upscale
🕐 10:00am – 11:00am seating 📍 4426 Central Ave, Camarillo ↗ 📞 805-988-9777 💰 $60 adults (mimosas included) · $25 kids 4–12 · Free under 3
Buffet Carving Station Mimosas Included Pastries & Desserts Family-Friendly

An all-you-can-eat Easter buffet with brunch favorites, a carving station, pastries, and desserts. The $60 per person price includes mimosas which makes it genuinely competitive with spots that charge separately for drinks. Kids 4–12 are $25, under 3 eat free.

Best for families who want a traditional Easter buffet feel with the mimosas already handled. Note the 10–11am seating window this one requires planning ahead.

Ottavio's Italian Restaurant
$$ Moderate
🕐 10:30am – 2:00pm 📍 1620 Ventura Blvd, Camarillo ↗ 📞 805-482-3810 💰 $37 adults · $19 kids 14 & under · Free under 5

A classic Easter brunch buffet with Italian favorites, breakfast dishes, a salad bar, and desserts. At $37 per adult it's one of the better values on this list and kids 5 and under eat free, which adds up for families. Ottavio's is a Camarillo staple for good reason.

Best for families and groups who want a reliable, traditional buffet at a fair price. A safe bet with something for everyone.

🕐 Open all day · Sunday April 5 📍 1755 E Daily Dr, Camarillo ↗ 📞 (805) 229-9164
Mexican Cuisine Bottomless Mimosas Live Mariachi No Reservations Required Open All Day

Rosa Madera brings a festive Easter Sunday with bottomless mimosas, breakfast options, and live mariachi all day long across all locations. The Camarillo location is open all day with no reservations required, making it a great option if you want a walk-in-friendly, lively atmosphere.

Best for groups and families who want a fun, festive Mexican Easter brunch with live music and a relaxed walk-in vibe. Great if you didn't plan ahead.
